ConsoleConnect is a social network dedicated to passionate gamers of PlayStation, Xbox, Switch, and PC. Users can chat 💬, share photos 📸, and interact with other players. The administrators provide the latest news for each console, game release dates 🎮, and a monthly TOP 5. There are also features to add/remove friends 👫 and a private mode 🔒. ConsoleConnect is the ideal place to connect gamers worldwide 🌍.
